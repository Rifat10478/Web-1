African Crane

The Grey Crowned Crane is famous for its striking golden "crown" of feathers on its head, which gives it a regal look. Its body is predominantly grey, with white wing feathers, black legs, and a white face. It also has a red throat patch beneath its chin.

African buffaloes are large, powerful animals. Males can weigh up to 2,000 pounds (900 kg), while females are generally smaller, weighing around 1,000 pounds (450 kg). They stand about 4.5 to 5 feet (1.4 to 1.5 meters) tall at the shoulder..
